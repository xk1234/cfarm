Social text automations choose among weighted post structures, then fill the
structure's constrained content slots. Proof-gated archetypes are excluded when
the automation does not provide evidence, and generated output is checked
against platform-specific rules before it is stored.

These registries do not contain curated visual examples, so this page focuses
on the automation direction, post structure, and exact settings.
