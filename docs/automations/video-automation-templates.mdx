Video automation templates are code-owned structural presets. They define the
ordered media segments, whether selected media plays in full, transition rules,
and the text direction attached to each segment or global overlay.

No synthetic examples are generated for this documentation. A template only
shows examples when a curated output is available; the current video preset
registry has none.
