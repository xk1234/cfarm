## Outcome

A reusable greenscreen meme clip is chroma-keyed over a randomly selected
astrology background image. A generated hook supplies the meme context, and the
rendered video can be posted through the generated-video publishing flow.

## Before you start

- A video collection containing at least one clean greenscreen reaction clip.
- An image collection containing captioned astrology backgrounds.
- Background images should make sense behind a person: natal charts, zodiac
  memes, constellation diagrams, or celestial scenes with sufficient empty
  space for the subject and text.
- A connected social account if the result will be posted from LumenClip.

## 1. Choose the format and collections

1. Open **New automation** → **Video**.
2. Select **Greenscreen Meme**.
3. Name it **Astrology Greenscreen Meme**.
4. Choose a video collection for **Greenscreen meme clip**.
5. Choose the astrology image collection for **Background image**.

![Greenscreen automation setup with video and image collections](/docs/workflows/greenscreen-01-setup.jpg)

The creation dialog blocks submission when either selected collection is empty.

## 2. Add astrology meme hooks

1. Open **Hooks**.
2. Replace generic hooks with topic-specific setups, for example:
   - `me checking their mercury sign after one confusing text`
   - `when the birth time changes the entire chart`
   - `trying to explain that a house is not a zodiac sign`
   - `when the fixed sign says they are totally flexible`
3. Keep hooks between roughly four and fourteen words so they remain readable
   above the subject.
4. Save casing and text style.

![Hooks page for the astrology greenscreen meme](/docs/workflows/greenscreen-02-hooks.jpg)

## 3. Configure schedule and publishing

1. Open **Schedule** and choose days, times, and timezone for recurring runs.
2. Open **Publishing** and add compatible accounts.
3. Choose **Manual**, **Review**, or **Auto**.
4. Check platform-specific disclosure and interaction controls.

![Publishing settings for the greenscreen automation](/docs/workflows/greenscreen-03-publishing.jpg)

## 4. Generate and inspect the video

1. Select **Create automation**.
2. Open the automation and select **Generate**.
3. Wait for the generated-video card to leave its queued/processing state.
4. Open the video viewer and watch the full loop.
5. Confirm:
   - the green background is removed cleanly;
   - the subject is not cropped;
   - the background supports the joke;
   - the hook is readable and does not cover the face;
   - the audio is expected and licensed.

## 5. Post the result

1. On the generated video card, select **Schedule post**.
2. Choose one or more compatible connected accounts.
3. Select **Draft**, **Publish in ~1 min**, or **Schedule**.
4. For scheduled posts, enter a future date and time.
5. Submit and verify the item in **Schedule**.

## Success check

- Both collections remain attached to the reusable automation.
- Each run can randomly select a valid background from the collection.
- The generated video is downloadable and has a publishing action.
- The calendar reflects scheduled content or the output is explicitly marked
  published after manual posting.
