## Outcome

A **React & Reveal** automation plays one anticipation/reaction clip in full,
then one reveal/demo video in full. The generated astrology video can be sent
to a connected account as a draft, published shortly, or scheduled.

## Before you start

- Put approved talking-head or AI UGC reaction clips in a **video collection**.
- Add the full reveal/demo video to the demo asset selector used by the format.
- Use vertical footage with audio rights cleared for every destination.
- Connect at least one compatible social account through PostFast.

## 1. Start from React & Reveal

1. Open **New automation**.
2. Select **Video**.
3. Choose **React & Reveal — Full reaction clip, then the full reveal**.

![Video format choices including React and Reveal](/docs/workflows/video-01-formats.jpg)

## 2. Name and create the automation

1. Name it **Astrology UGC Reaction**.
2. The creation dialog explains that media is configured after creation.
3. Open **Hooks**, **Schedule**, and **Publishing** before selecting **Create
   automation**.

![React and Reveal automation setup](/docs/workflows/ugc-reaction-01-setup.jpg)

> **Setup warning:** React & Reveal does not currently validate its required
> reaction collection and reveal asset in this first dialog. Configure both in
> **Video Format** immediately after creation. The roadmap tracks stronger
> first-run validation.

## 3. Configure full-length media

1. After creation, open **Video Format**.
2. For **Anticipation**, choose **Collection**, media type **Video**, and select
   the reaction collection. The selected video plays in full; there are no
   clip-count, seconds-per-clip, or cut controls for this segment.
3. For **Reveal**, choose the demo video. It also plays in full.
4. Confirm the hook describes a reveal the second video actually pays off.

Example concept:

- Anticipation hook: `the placement that changes how everyone reads you`
- Reveal: a short natal-chart walkthrough showing where to find the rising sign
- Payoff caption: `your ascendant is the sign rising on the eastern horizon at
your birth time`

## 4. Configure hooks

1. Open **Hooks & Style**.
2. Add several specific, reusable curiosity hooks.
3. Keep the text short enough to read before the full reaction clip ends.
4. Save the hook casing and text style.

![Hooks configuration for the astrology reaction automation](/docs/workflows/ugc-reaction-02-hooks.jpg)

## 5. Configure social publishing

1. Open **Social Media Settings**.
2. Select **Add accounts** and choose the intended destinations.
3. Choose one publishing workflow:
   - **Manual** — remind you to post;
   - **Review** — require approval before publishing;
   - **Auto** — schedule generated content automatically.
4. Review the provider-specific controls. For TikTok, decide whether the upload
   is a draft, whether TikTok chooses music, and whether comments, duet, stitch,
   AI-content disclosure, or brand disclosure apply.

![Publishing settings for a React and Reveal automation](/docs/workflows/ugc-reaction-03-publishing.jpg)

## 6. Generate, review, and post

1. Select **Create automation**, then open its **Overview**.
2. Select **Generate**. A manual generation remains unpublished and unscheduled.
3. Play the complete rendered video. Verify the reaction and reveal are not
   trimmed and the reveal pays off the hook.
4. On the generated video card, select the calendar/**Schedule post** control.
5. Choose connected accounts and select **Draft**, **Publish in ~1 min**, or
   **Schedule**.
6. Submit the post and verify its status in **Schedule**.

## Success check

- Both source videos play in full.
- Text does not obstruct faces or the reveal.
- The generated video card exposes download and posting controls.
- The selected account receives a draft, immediate post, or scheduled post.
